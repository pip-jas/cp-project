About

Hello, I am a Motion Designer based in Austin, Tx.

I am very passionate about design, art, music and film as my primary elements of communication and how we are able to combine these in order to convey a bigger message and use it as a powerful tool for new ways of communication.

I am also interested in working in physical spaces where visuals, light and sound combine. I have participated doing visuals for local musical appearances in Austin, Tx. I have also collaborated with AMODA (Austin Museum of Digital Art) with the project Loopmass and Loopmass: Advanced Plants, where more than 50 artists in the digital art industry have participated and collaborated in teams for a 2 night event, both events with different concepts and different environment.
